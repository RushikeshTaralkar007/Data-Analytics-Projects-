
# 📊 Telecom Customer Churn Analysis

A comprehensive **Data Analytics** project that analyzes customer churn behavior in a telecom company using **Python**, **Pandas**, and **Power BI**. The project focuses on identifying customer churn patterns, understanding customer demographics, analyzing service usage, contract and payment behavior, and providing actionable business insights through interactive dashboards.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges in the telecom industry. Retaining existing customers is often more cost-effective than acquiring new ones. This project transforms raw customer data into meaningful insights using data cleaning, analysis, and interactive visualizations.

The dashboard helps answer key business questions such as:

- Which customers are most likely to churn?
- Which contract types have the highest churn?
- How do internet services influence customer retention?
- What is the impact of payment methods on churn?
- How do customer demographics affect churn?

---

## 📊 Key Business Insights

- Customers with **Month-to-Month contracts** exhibit the highest churn.
- **Fiber Optic** users have a higher churn rate compared to DSL users.
- Customers using **Automatic Payment Methods** tend to have better retention.
- Customers with **Online Security** and **Tech Support** services are less likely to churn.
- Long-term customers contribute significantly higher lifetime revenue.
