
# 🚖 OLA Ride Analytics

## Project Overview

The **OLA Ride Analytics** project analyzes one month of ride booking data to uncover business insights related to bookings, cancellations, revenue, customer behavior, and ride performance. The project demonstrates an end-to-end data analytics workflow using **SQL** and **Power BI**.

The objective is to transform raw ride data into meaningful insights that can support operational improvements and data-driven business decisions.

---

## Business Problem

Ride-hailing companies generate large volumes of operational data every day. Analyzing this data helps answer key business questions such as:

* How many rides were successfully completed?
* What are the major reasons for ride cancellations?
* Which vehicle types generate the highest revenue?
* How do customer ratings compare with driver ratings?
* Which payment methods are most frequently used?
* What are the peak booking periods?

## Business Insights

* Identified the most frequently used vehicle categories.
* Analyzed ride cancellation trends from both customers and drivers.
* Evaluated revenue generated by different ride types.
* Measured customer satisfaction using rating analysis.
* Identified peak booking periods for operational planning.
* Compared ride performance across booking statuses.

---

## Recommendations

* Reduce ride cancellations by analyzing common cancellation reasons.
* Improve driver allocation during peak demand periods.
* Promote high-performing vehicle categories.
* Enhance customer experience by monitoring ratings.
* Optimize payment options based on customer preferences.
